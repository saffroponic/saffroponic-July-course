FAQ

Questions Answered

I have zero farming experience. Is this training for me?
This training was specifically designed for complete beginners. No prior knowledge needed. If you can plug in a fridge, you can do this. We've trained homemakers, software engineers, retired professionals, and students — all with zero farming background.
I don't live in Kashmir — can I still grow saffron?
Absolutely. That's exactly what Saffroponic solves. Because we use a climate-controlled environment, it doesn't matter if you're in Chennai, Mumbai, or Rajasthan. If you have soil, water, electricity, and space for a fridge — you can grow Kashmiri saffron anywhere in India.
Which variety of saffron can be grown with the Saffroponic kit?
We use authentic Kashmiri saffron corms (Crocus sativus) — the same variety that sells at ₹3–5 lakh per kg in the Indian market and ₹6–10 lakh per kg in the export market. No compromise on quality or variety.
How many kg of seeds fit in a standard 300L fridge?
A 300L cold drink fridge can hold 5 to 7 kg of saffron corms — a solid beginner batch to learn, practice, calculate your returns, and build confidence before scaling up.
How much saffron can one fridge actually produce?
From 5–7 kg of corms in a 300L fridge, you can harvest approximately 5–7 grams of pure dried saffron — worth ₹1,500 to ₹3,500 at retail market price. And that's just your first practice batch. Each season, your corms multiply — meaning your yield grows without buying new seeds.
